About Us

who We Are


NiV is a leading hybrid social enterprise in Northern Ghana committed to empowering young people through innovation, entrepreneurship, and sustainable development. By combining social impact with business-driven solutions, NiV creates opportunities that help youth become economically independent and self-reliant. Through strategic partnerships with impact investors, development agencies, and like-minded organizations, NiV continues to drive positive transformation across underserved communities.

Our work spans multiple sectors including skills development, transport, health, hospitality, agriculture, waste management, and climate action. We believe that sustainable growth comes from equipping young people with practical skills, access to resources, and opportunities to participate meaningfully in the economy. NiV therefore focuses not only on training and capacity building but also on creating pathways to employment, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

At the heart of our approach is a strong commitment to market access and business support services. We support startups, youth-led enterprises, and community initiatives with mentorship, networking opportunities, business development support, and access to markets that enable them to grow sustainably. By fostering innovation and collaboration, NiV helps build resilient businesses that create jobs, strengthen local economies, and deliver long-term social impact.

Driven by a vision of inclusive and sustainable development, NiV is shaping a future where young people are empowered to lead change, solve community challenges, and contribute to a thriving and environmentally responsible society.,
